Before we find out if you were approved for a mortgage, you should know about the Park Forest Commission on Human Relations.

When a Negro family moves into Park Forest, the Commission visits and asks the family several questions. Then the Commission explains to other residents that a Negro family is moving into the area.

Your neighbors are more likely to welcome you into the community if you answer the questions. »
