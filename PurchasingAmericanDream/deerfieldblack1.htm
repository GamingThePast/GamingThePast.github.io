There are some things you should know about financing a home before we visit Deerfield.

You need mortgage credit to buy a house, but there are several financial standards you must meet in order to qualify for that credit. You must make sufficient income to meet the down payment and monthly payment requirements. The property you are purchasing must be of good quality and location.

Racial considerations also play a role in whether or not you qualify. »
