Frank Derrick, a resident of Park Terrace, says:

I'm a postman. I make about $4,000 a year. My wife, Geraldene, and I moved here from the South Side. We've got a brick ranch with three bedrooms, two TV sets, an air conditioner, a piano, a 1953 Chrysler, and the best lawn on the block. A lot of Negroes never think too much about their lawns in the city, but when they come out here they really put all of themselves into their homes.
Your chances of getting a mortgage in Markham are good. »
