Deerfield

Located 23 miles northwest of Chicago, Deerfield offers its 11,786 residents split-level homes at an average price of $23,000. Most residents are college graduates, and make an average of $9,000 a year. The Edens Expressway was completed in 1959, providing greater access to the downtown area for commuters traveling to work by car.
