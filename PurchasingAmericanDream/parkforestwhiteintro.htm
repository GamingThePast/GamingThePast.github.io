Park Forest

Located 30 miles south of the Loop, Park Forest is a community of 27,372 residents living primarily in 5-room ranch-style houses. The people of Park Forest are in their early twenties and well-educated, most having completed some college. Their median income, at $8,943, reflects this fairly high level of education. The median value of a home in Park Forest is $17,500. Almost half of the residents moved into their home within the last two years.
